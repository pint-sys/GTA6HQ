Is GTA6HQ an official Rockstar Games website?
No. GTA6HQ is an independent, unofficial fan community site. We're not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive.
When does GTA 6 release?
Rockstar has confirmed November 19, 2026 for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. See the countdown at the top of the page for the exact time remaining.
Is GTA 6 coming to PC?
Not yet announced. Rockstar has historically released PC ports 12–18 months after the console launch, but nothing official has been confirmed for GTA 6 PC as of this writing.
Where do the news articles on this site come from?
Our News section automatically pulls from established gaming outlets (IGN, Eurogamer, PC Gamer, Kotaku, Rock Paper Shotgun, GameSpot) filtered for GTA 6-related coverage, refreshed automatically throughout the day.
Can I contribute job guides or ask questions?
Yes — anyone can post a question in the Q&A section. Job guide submissions are currently curated by our admin team to keep quality and accuracy high, alongside guides automatically verified from public sources.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
